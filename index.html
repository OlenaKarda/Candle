SCENTED CANDLES

The only thing that is better than a beautiful candle is a beautiful scented candle. Our candles have a range of different scents. From fresh linen to a walk in the woods in the fall. Our scented candles are made from rapeseed wax, an eco friendly alternative to paraffin.

HANDMADE Candles

During the pandemic we discovered a new hobby: making candles. At first we started by reusing old candles, but soon we decided we wanted to do it differently. Every candle is eco-friendly en hand poured with a lot of love. We are based in the Netherlands, but ship worldwide.

CANDLES & HOMEWARE

Soon after starting to make candles we found that just making candles wasn’t enough. We wanted something different for our vessels, but couldn’t find what we were looking for. So we decided to make them ourselves. Our concrete vessels are a hit with our customers and they were asking for more. So slowly we are adding new concrete homeware to the shop. Get yours now!

SPECIAL OCCASSIONS

Getting married and looking for party favors? Having a gender reveal or baby shower? Someone you love just needs to hear a special message? It’s all possible here at Canndle. Just shoot us a message through our contact form and we’ll gladly look at the options with you.

SOY WAX VS. RAPESEED WAX
Candles can be made from different types of wax. A lot of candles are made from paraffin or stearin. But with the growing number of people wanting to be sustainable and plant-based these candles are not the best option. Soy wax and rapeseed wax are both plant-based types of wax. But there are a few differences between them.
